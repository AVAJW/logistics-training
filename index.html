Shipper Initiates Load

The process begins when a shipper contacts us with a need to move goods, providing details like origin, destination, commodity, and delivery timelines.

Finding the Right Carrier

We leverage our network and load boards to find a suitable carrier with available capacity that matches the shipper's requirements.

Rate Confirmation & Booking

Once a carrier is selected, we negotiate rates and send a comprehensive rate confirmation document outlining all load details.

Real-time Shipment Tracking

Throughout the journey, we use advanced tracking tools to monitor the shipment's progress and provide timely updates to the shipper.

Successful Delivery & POD

Upon successful delivery, the carrier obtains a Proof of Delivery (POD), which is then sent to us to confirm completion.

Billing and Payment Processing

Finally, we invoice the shipper for our services and ensure the carrier is promptly paid, completing the load cycle.
